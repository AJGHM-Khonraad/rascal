# Languages
:concept: Languages

.Synopsis
Definitions of several languages and their tools.

.Syntax

.Types

.Function

.Description

Examples of several languages and the implementation of tools like interpreters and compilers:

** <<Languages-Exp,Exp>>: The _hello world_ of syntax definition and language definition.
*** <<Exp-Abstract,Abstract>>: A version of Exp based on abstract syntax.
*** <<Exp-Combined,Combined>>: Combine concrete syntax with abstract syntax.
*** <<Exp-Concrete,Concrete>>: Various versions of Exp based on concrete syntax.
** <<Languages-Func,Func>>: Func is a tiny functional language; we present several interpreters for it.
*** <<Func-AbstractSyntax,Abstract Syntax>>: The abstract syntax for Func.
*** <<Func-ConcreteSyntax,Concrete Syntax>>: The concrete syntax of Func.
*** <<Func-Eval0,Eval0>>: A Func interpreter that does not support let-expressions and pointers.
*** <<Func-Eval1,Eval1>>: .
*** <<Func-Eval2,Eval2>>: .
*** <<Func-Eval3,Eval3>>: .
*** <<Func-LoadAST,Load AST>>: Parse Func program from string or file and convert to an abstract syntax tree.
*** <<Func-Parse,Parse>>: Parse a Func program from a string or a file.
** <<Languages-Lisra,Lisra>>: A lisp interpreter in Rascal.
*** <<Lisra-Eval,Eval>>: A Lisp interpreter.
*** <<Lisra-Parse,Parse>>: Parsing a Lisp expression.
*** <<Lisra-Pretty,Pretty>>: A Lisp pretty printer.
*** <<Lisra-Runtime,Runtime>>: The runtime representation of Lisp programs and data.
*** <<Lisra-Syntax,Syntax>>: The textual syntax of Lisp.
*** <<Lisra-Test,Test>>: Tests for the Lisp interpreter.
** <<Languages-Pico,Pico>>: The classical toy language, including a specialized IDE.
*** <<Pico-Abstract,Abstract>>: Abstract syntax for Pico.
*** <<Pico-Assembly,Assembly>>: Assembly language for Pico.
*** <<Pico-Compile,Compile>>: Compile a Pico program to assembly language.
*** <<Pico-ControlFlow,ControlFlow>>: Compute the control flow graph for a Pico program.
*** <<Pico-Evaluate,Evaluate>>: Evaluate a Pico program.
*** <<Pico-IDE,IDE>>: An Integrated Development Environment for Pico.
*** <<Pico-Load,Load>>: Convert a Pico parse tree into a Pico abstract syntax tree.
*** <<Pico-Syntax,Syntax>>: Concrete syntax for Pico.
*** <<Pico-Typecheck,Typecheck>>: Typechecker a Pico program.
*** <<Pico-Uninit,Uninit>>: Find unitialized variables in a Pico program.
*** <<Pico-UseDef,UseDef>>: Compute use-def information for the variables in a Pico program.
*** <<Pico-Visualize,Visualize>>: Visualize Pico Control Flow Graphs.

Other languages that we are considering (but are not yet described):

*  Oberon0: a scaled down version of the Oberon language.
*  MissGrant: a state machine language.
